My (Digital) Literacy Story

Create your own literacy narrative that tells of a significant experience you had with (digital) literacy in either your personal or professional life. Digital is in parenthesis because while it would be great if it were a story of your experience with digital technology, don't let that limit you. For example, you might like to tell your story of developing a non-digital literacy in a digital format. It could be in the form of a simple word processed document or a more ambitious multimedia format.

To Do:

  1. Write or record your (digital) literacy narrative. (You might like to browse the DLNA for more inspiring stories like Mark and John's.)
  2. Add a new discussion post and paste in, or attach your story.
  3. Optionally, consider depositing your digital literacy story with the Digital Archive of Literacy Narratives.

Examples for "My (Digital) Literacy Story":
Mark Marino: The birth of WriterResponseTheory.org

John Scenters-Zapico: The impact composing with a computer had on my life
