Emergency Rations

Think about a digital technology you can't live without. The kind of thing you might return home to retrieve if you had forgotten. It doesn't have to be a physical object, it could be a website or an app, or even a digital practise. Be creative. You might like to browse David Hopkins' ‘Emergency rations’ book for ideas.

To Do:

  1. Add a new discussion post and write about your use of your ‘Emergency rations’ and why you consider it essential.
  2. Take a selfie of yourself using the technology and add it to your post. (If you aren't comfortable sharing a selfie, try taking a creative image of the technology, or of what it allows you to do).
  3. Optionally, Create a flickr account and upload your photo with the tags #EdTechRations and #TERTL509.
