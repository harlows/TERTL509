My Personal Technology Timeline

Create your own timeline that displays your digital technology history.

To Do:

  1. Start by brainstorming your personal and professional digital milestones, some key milestones might include:
    • First Computer
    • First PowerPoint presentation to a conference or class
    • Internet Connection
    • First Mobile/Smart Phone
    • Wearable Technology
    • Gaming consoles
    • Smart home devices
  2. Consider how you might present your timeline. It can be as simple as a two column table, or like our exemplars, you might like to experiment with an online timeline.
    • Canva
    • Knight Lab's Timeline (which was used for the H5P Timeline activity).
    • Time.Graphics
  3. Add a new discussion post and add, attach or embed your timeline.
