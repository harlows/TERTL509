Visitors and Residents

A Visitors and Residents (V&R) map is a tool used to understand how individuals engage with the digital world. It helps visualise the different ways people use the internet and digital technologies.

To Do:

  1. Download the Visitors & Residents template.
  2. Read the Wikipedia page on the Visitor and Resident model for more background on the process watch: the video.
  3. Look at some of the exemplar maps for ideas here and on flickr.
  4. Create your own Visitors and Residents map using your choice of technology. You could:
    • Draw on paper and take a pic with your phone.
    • Use a drawing app like Canva.
    • Use a presentation app like PowerPoint.
  5. Add a new discussion post and embed or attach your map.
  6. Consider sharing it more widely on flickr.
