Bookmark This

To Do:

When you have found a digital literacy resource you would like to bookmark and share, return to this activity:
  1. Add a new discussion topic below
    • Copy and paste the web address (or URL) into the Subject field.
    • In the Message field, briefly explain why you think this resource is valuable.

To make sure you can take your bookmarks with you, rather than posting them in Moodle, you can use a social bookmarking service. Raindrop.io is a bookmark manager that allows you to take your bookmarks everywhere, and share them easily.

  1. Create an account with Raindrop.io
  2. Login and click on the + Add button in the top right and bookmark at least one digital literacy resource (it can be the same one you posted or something new).
  3. Hover over the bookmark and click the Edit button. Write a brief note explaining why you think the resource is valuable.
  4. Tag it with appropriate keywords.
  5. Click on the Collection dropdown menu and search for the TERTL509 collection to add your bookmark.
You might like to simplify adding bookmarks by adding the Raindrop.io extension to your browser.
