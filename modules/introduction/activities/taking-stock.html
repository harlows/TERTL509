Taking Stock

Complete the Digital Competence Scale for University Teachers (DCS-UT) questionnaire while thinking about your use of digital tools, practises, and strategies for engaging students through technology. This will help you take stock of where you are now and help you focus on where you want to develop.

To Do:

  1. Complete the Digital Competence Scale for University Teachers (DCS-UT) questionnaire. You can either:
    • Fill out the online form and have your results emailed, or
    • Download the questionnaire and score it yourself.
  2. Add a new discussion post reflecting on your digital journey, what you learned from the survey, and how you plan to improve your skills.
  3. Write a letter to your future self asking if you have met the digital literacy goal you have set for yourself.

This activity is part of the Improving Your Digital Literacy section of the Introduction Module.
