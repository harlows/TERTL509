Hypothes.ising

Hypothes.is is a great tool for collaboratively annotating web pages and online PDFs.

To Do:

  1. Sign up for a free hypothes.is account.
  2. Add the hypothes.is Chrome/Edge browser extension or the Firefox browser bookmarklet.
  3. Join the TERTL509 group.
  4. Familiarise yourself with Annotation Basics.
  5. Read Mark Brown's A Critical Review of Frameworks for Digital Literacy series of blog posts and make some annotations:
    • Highlight and annotate a relevant section. Don't forget to tag it TERTL509!
    • If you'd rather not make your annotation public, post it to the TERTL509 group by clicking on the group dropdown menu in the web app.
    • Try replying to someone else's annotation.
  6. Add a new discussion topic and paste in the links to your annotations (you can get the links by clicking on the share icon on the bottom righthand corner of the annotation).
